Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround times vary by service: Business cards (same day), Brochures (2-3 days), Custom t-shirts (3-5 days), Large orders (5-7 days). Rush orders available for urgent requests.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our experienced design team can help create professional designs for your printing projects. We offer basic design assistance as well as complete design services.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, AI, PSD, CDR, JPG, PNG, and TIFF files. For best results, we recommend PDF files with 300 DPI resolution and CMYK color mode.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer competitive pricing and bulk discounts for large orders. Contact us with your quantity requirements for a custom quote.

Can I see a sample before placing a large order?

Absolutely! We can provide samples of your design before proceeding with the full order. This ensures you're completely satisfied with the quality and design.
